Find What’s Eating Your Storage in 1 Minute

Hard drive full? Windows updates failing? Find the biggest folders and delete junk fast. No extra software needed for basic cleanup.

Windows 11/10 Steps:

  1. Click Start Menu → Settings
  2. Click "System" → "Storage"
  3. Wait 10-30 seconds. Windows scans your drive and shows a breakdown.
  4. Click each category: "Apps & features", "Documents", "Pictures", "Temporary files"
  5. Click "Temporary files" → Check all boxes → "Remove files". This alone frees 5-20GB instantly. Safe to delete.
  6. Go back → Click "Other" to see huge folders you forgot about. Old game installs, downloads, videos.

Pro Tip: TreeSize Free

For a visual map: Google "TreeSize Free" → Download from jam-software.com. It shows your entire hard drive as boxes. Biggest folder = biggest box. You can instantly see if "Downloads" or "Videos" is eating 200GB.

Quick Wins: Empty Recycle Bin, uninstall games you don't play, delete old phone backups in iTunes, clear "Downloads" folder.

Mac Users:

Apple Menu → About This Mac → Storage → Manage → Apple gives you recommendations. "Reduce Clutter" is the best one.
