How to Check Internet Speed: Ping Google in 10 Seconds

1. Press Win + R, type cmd, hit Enter.
2. Type ping google.com -t and press Enter.
3. Look for "time=" — under 50ms is great, over 150ms means laggy.

Is Your Laptop Overheating? Check Temperature Now

Method 1: Open Task Manager Ctrl + Shift + Esc → Performance tab.
Method 2: Download free tool HWInfo or Speccy for exact CPU/GPU temps. Under 80°C is safe.
