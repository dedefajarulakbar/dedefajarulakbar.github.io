What I Learned

Scrub Daddy is my workhorse. In cold water, it's hard enough to scrape burnt rice from pans without scratching. In warm water, it softens for plates. It lasted over 4 months before I replaced it — my old regular sponge died in 2 weeks.

Scrub Mommy is the gentle one. The yellow ResoFoam side is perfect for glasses and non-stick cookware. It never smells because the foam dries fast. I keep one at the sink for quick counter wipes.

My Verdict: Get both, but for different jobs. Use Daddy for pots and tough mess, Mommy for everything else. If you must pick one, choose Mommy — it's more versatile for daily use and stays odor-free longer.
Pro Tip: I cut each sponge in half — one half for dishes, one for sink/counter. Lasts 2x longer and you always have a fresh side. Rinse well, squeeze dry, and microwave damp for 60 seconds weekly.

Care Tips (Makes Them Last 4 Months)

  • Rinse well and squeeze dry after each use — don't leave sitting in water
  • Microwave damp sponge 60 seconds weekly to kill bacteria
  • Dishwasher top rack once a week
  • Store upright on edge, not flat in sink
